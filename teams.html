Our Programs

Discover our impactful programs working tirelessly to create positive change in communities across India.

Nehru Yuva Kendra

During the year 2025, awareness and motivation sessions were conducted in 112 government schools focusing on the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, child behaviour, safety awareness, and personality development. These sessions aimed to educate and empower students with essential knowledge regarding child protection, appropriate behaviour, and personal well-being. Additionally, block-level National Building elocution competitions were organized at the Women’s Degree College, providing a platform for young students to showcase their communication skills and confidence. Two students, Deena Singh and Maheswari, were selected to represent the block at the district level. Among them, Deena Singh further excelled and was selected to participate at the state level, bringing pride and recognition to the institution.

Swachh Bharat clean India Awareness programme

In 2021, she actively participated in the Swachh Bharat (Clean India) Programme, contributing to cleanliness and public hygiene awareness initiatives. As part of the campaign, she took part in activities aimed at educating communities about the importance of sanitation, environmental cleanliness, and maintaining healthy surroundings. Her involvement reflected her commitment to social responsibility and community welfare.

Indian red cross society life member

As a life member of the Indian Red Cross Society, she actively extended humanitarian support during flood situations by assisting affected communities with relief efforts. Her contribution included providing timely help and support to those in distress, reflecting her compassion, commitment to social service, and dedication to helping people during times of crisis.

Social Service award in 2 covid waves

On 15th August 2021, she was honored with the Best Social Service Award in recognition of her dedicated service during the second wave of the COVID-19 pandemic. The award was presented by J.C. Shri Dinesh Kumar A.S. in appreciation of her selfless efforts, humanitarian service, and unwavering commitment to supporting people during one of the most challenging public health crises.

Best Youth Club Award

She was honored with the District Level Best Youth Club Award, presented by the State Officer of Nehru Yuva Kendra, in recognition of her outstanding contribution to youth development, social service, and active participation in community welfare initiatives. This achievement reflects her leadership, dedication, and commitment to empowering youth and creating a positive impact in society.

Blood Donation Camps

In 2019, blood grouping camps were successfully organized across Anganwadi schools, primary schools, colleges, and block-level areas as part of a community health awareness initiative. These camps aimed to create awareness about the importance of knowing one’s blood group, promoting health consciousness, and encouraging preparedness during medical emergencies. The initiative reflected a strong commitment to public health service and community welfare.
