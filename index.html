About Prajwala Community Development Society:
Prajwala Community Development Society is a service organization dedicated to community development. The organization conducts various initiatives—including women's empowerment, youth development, sanitation, education, and social service programs—while simultaneously raising public awareness regarding these issues. Striving for the upliftment of marginalized sections of society, the organization undertakes numerous charitable activities.

With the specific objective of assisting cancer patients, the organization organizes hair donation drives. As part of this initiative, hair donations have been successfully facilitated on two separate occasions. By continuing this charitable endeavor, the organization serves as an inspiration to many others.

The organization also takes the lead in raising awareness regarding sanitation and hygiene. In recognition of its contributions to the 'Swachh Bharat' (Clean India) campaign, it received awards consecutively in the years 2018, 2019, and 2020. With social service as its core mission, Prajwala Community Development Society continues to successfully implement a wide range of charitable programs.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to justice, healthcare, education, and dignified livelihood — regardless of gender, caste, or economic background.

  • Women Empowerment: Free tailoring centers, self-defense training, and legal aid for women.
  • Health for All: Blood donation camps, COVID relief, and accessible medical support.
  • Environmental Sustainability: Tree planting drives and awareness campaigns.

Our Mission

"To serve as a pillar of support for the distressed, the illiterate, and the elderly — ensuring their rights, dignity, and well-being."

  • Legal Aid: Para-Legal services through District Legal Services Authority to ensure justice for all.
  • Food Security: Distributed essentials to 50,000+ families during COVID-19 lockdown.
  • Youth Development: Personality development programs and awards for young volunteers.

Selfless Service

Driven by a spirit of voluntary service, putting others' needs before our own — regardless of personal circumstances.

Social Justice

Standing against age-old inequalities and ensuring every individual has access to their rightful entitlements and legal aid.

Women's Safety

Empowering women through self-defense training, legal protection, and economic independence via skill development.

Environmental Care

Planting thousands of saplings and fighting pollution — because caring for nature is caring for the future.
